Trauma-Informed Principles

Safety

Describe topics in advance, incorporate frequent check-ins, and create predictable structures.

Trust & Accountability

Be transparent about processes, follow through on commitments, and avoid overpromising.

Collaboration & Equity

Include marginalized voices, offer flexible participation methods, and ensure genuine partnership.

Empowerment

Provide meaningful choices, validate feelings, and avoid overwhelming participants.

Hope

Highlight positive impact, show how input matters, and focus on future benefits.
